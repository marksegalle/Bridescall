  • Set up your wedding registry with these easy steps

  • Step 1

    Start Your Registry

    Click on the “Create Registry” button at the top-right corner of your screen.

  • Step 2

    Answer Our Registry Wizard

    Answer a series of questions about the details of your wedding. Please note that the information you give will be used as part of your e-invite, which you can create on this website also.

  • Step 3

    Sign Up to Save the Information

    Set up an account with us so you can have your wedding registry set and ready.

  • Step 4

    Customize Your Wedding Registry

    Change your cover photo or profile picture depending on your preference. The more personalized, the more appealing.

  • Step 5

    Send Your Registry to Guests

    Send out your wedding registry link to guests and loved ones, whether in the Philippines or abroad. Make your gift list as extensive as you want it to be.

  • Step 6

    Receive the Gifts You Want

    Sit back, relax, and receive the gifts from your generous guests and loved ones!
